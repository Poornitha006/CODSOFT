About the System

The Subject Allocation System is designed to simplify the management of subject assignments in schools, colleges, and universities. Administrators can allocate subjects to students and faculty based on predefined criteria such as class schedules, faculty availability, and student preferences.

Key Features

Automated Allocation

Efficiently assigns subjects to students and faculty using a streamlined process.

Custom Rules

Allows administrators to set specific rules for allocation based on requirements.

User Portals

Separate portals for students and faculty to view their subject assignments and schedules.

Conflict Resolution

Identifies and resolves scheduling conflicts during the allocation process.

Reports and Analytics

Generates detailed reports on subject allocation for better decision-making.

Real-Time Updates

Keeps users informed of changes in allocations and schedules instantly.
